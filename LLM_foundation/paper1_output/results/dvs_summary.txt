
============================================================
DVS 분석 최종 요약
============================================================

■ 분석 대상: 2177건 (판독문 있고 miss 판정 가능한 건)
■ 전체 Miss rate: 707/2177 (32.5%)

■ Multivariable Model:
  C-statistic (AUC): 0.761
  Optimism-corrected AUC: 0.757
  Bootstrap 95% CI: (0.757-0.762)

■ Significant predictors (p<0.05):
  MRI (vs CE CT): aOR 1.30 (1.00-1.69), p=0.048
  Entirely normal report: aOR 7.53 (4.84-11.71), p=<0.001
  Mass/lesion mentioned: aOR 0.57 (0.45-0.72), p=<0.001
  No differential diagnosis: aOR 1.79 (1.43-2.24), p=<0.001
  Clinical info provided: aOR 0.32 (0.25-0.40), p=<0.001
